Project Overview

SafeSocial is a digital safety platform designed to protect teenagers from online threats such as cyberbullying, harassment, and interactions with online predators. The platform monitors social media interactions and alerts users when harmful behavior is detected while educating teens about safe online practices.

Target Users

  • Teenagers aged 13-19 who actively use social media
  • Parents and guardians who want to ensure their children's online safety
  • Schools and counselors supporting student wellbeing
  • Youth organizations promoting digital responsibility

Key Features

Cyberbullying Detection

AI detects abusive language and alerts users when harmful communication occurs.

Predator Detection

Identifies suspicious behavior patterns and warns users about unsafe interactions.

Panic Button

Quickly report harassment, block users, and save evidence of harmful interactions.

Privacy Alerts

Warns teens before they share sensitive personal information online.

Safety Education

Interactive lessons and tips that teach teens responsible social media behavior.

Product Mockups

Technology Used

  • Frontend: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React
  • Backend: Node.js or Python
  • Database: MongoDB / PostgreSQL
  • Security: Data encryption and secure authentication
  • Integration with social media APIs

AI Component

SafeSocial uses Artificial Intelligence and Natural Language Processing (NLP) to analyze messages and comments. The AI detects cyberbullying, harassment, and suspicious patterns that may indicate grooming or predatory behavior. When risks are detected, the system sends alerts and suggests actions such as blocking or reporting users.
